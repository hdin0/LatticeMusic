More Information BOID MUSIC

This project was born out of the idea to use the bottom-up theory of music to create a new method of algorithmic composition. We assigned to each position a specific pitch, where each pitch was obtained from the results of our numerical simulations. As the boids travel through the domain, we reobtain the pitch values, and that is what is played. Visually, the colors represent a particular pitch index from 0 to 11 (A twelve note system). The pitch indices relate to each other in the traditional western sense. For example, 0 to 4 is a major third and 1 to 5 is also a major third ()
Descriptions of each control field can be seen by hovering over either the text, or text field area.
